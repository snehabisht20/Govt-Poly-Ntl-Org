Say No to Ragging

About the Campaign

At Government Polytechnic Nainital, we are committed to eradicating ragging and fostering a safe, inclusive campus environment. Our anti-ragging campaign educates students about the legal and social consequences of ragging, supports victims, and promotes harmony among the student community.
