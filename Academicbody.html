Academic Body

Government Polytechnic Nainital (GPN) is a technology-oriented institution with a strong emphasis on holistic education. The academic framework encourages all-round development, nurturing students to tackle professional challenges efficiently from day one on campus.

The syllabus is meticulously designed and regularly updated to align with industry standards, ensuring students receive relevant technical education with strong professional prospects.

The Academic Body and Advisory Council collaborate to monitor, revise, and recommend a curriculum that adheres to current industry needs. Comprising experienced academicians, subject authorities, industry experts, and reputed educationists, these bodies establish the foundation for quality education at GPN.
