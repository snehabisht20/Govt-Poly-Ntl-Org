About Us

Government Polytechnic Nainital, established in 1957, is a premier technical institute in Uttarakhand, dedicated to providing high-quality education and fostering innovation. Affiliated with the Uttarakhand Board of Technical Education, Roorkee, the institute offers diploma programs in various engineering and technical disciplines, equipping students with practical skills and theoretical knowledge to excel in their careers.

Genesis

Founded in 1957, Government Polytechnic Nainital has a rich history of over six decades, evolving from a modest technical school to a leading polytechnic institute in Uttarakhand. Established to meet the growing demand for skilled technicians in post-independence India, the institute has consistently adapted its curriculum to align with industry needs, fostering innovation and entrepreneurship among its students.
