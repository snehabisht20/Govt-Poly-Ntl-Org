HOSTEL LIKE A HOME

Hostel is provided for GIRLS and BOYS inside the campus. Students are provided with adequate facilities to make them feel at home. Their stay in the hostel enables them to imbibe a different lifestyle and involve them in activities including Yoga, Meditation, Sports, Music, etc., suiting individual tastes and preferences.

GPN HOSTEL IS EQUIPPED WITH:

  • 24 hours electricity and water supply
  • Green refreshing surroundings to relax
  • First aid facilities and visit of doctors on call
  • Airy and well-furnished rooms
  • Hygienic, tasty, and nourishing food for inmates

HOSTEL RULES:

  • Hostel fee is to be paid in advance during the start of each session.
  • Hostel fee is non-refundable in case of expulsion from the hostel by management.
  • Shifting of furniture from one room to another is not allowed.
  • No interchange of rooms is permitted without written permission from the hostel warden.
  • Students are not allowed to stay in hostel rooms during class timings.
  • Morning assembly and exercises are compulsory for all hostlers; however, relaxation/exemption may be given in case of physical disability on a case-by-case basis.

HOSTEL FEE

  • Hostel fees are determined annually. Please contact the administration for current rates.
